CUTE RABBIT

Rabbits have long ears, a short tail, long hind legs and they continuously growing incisors.
Most species are gray or brown and range in size from 10 to 18 and long is (25 to 45 cm).
Their reproductive rate is very high; unlike hares, rabbits are born blind, hairless, and helpless.
Most species are nocturnal and live alone in burrows.
This species is the ancestor to all domestic breeds.
They feed primarily on grasses. Encyclopedia Britannica
